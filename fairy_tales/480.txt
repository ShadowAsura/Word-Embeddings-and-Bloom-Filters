       "Poussie, Poussie Baudrons,       Where hae ye been?"     "I've been at London,       Seeing the Queen!"      "Poussie, Poussie Baudrons,       What got ye there?"     "I got a guid fat mousikie,       Rinning up a stair."      "Poussie, Poussie Baudrons,       What did ye do wi't?"     "I put it in my meal-poke       To eat it to my bread."
